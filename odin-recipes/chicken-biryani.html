Chicken Biryani

Traditional chicken biryani marinated chickenat the bottom of a pot

followed by another layer of par cooked rick, herbs, saffron infused milk & ghee

Ingredients :

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Fried Onions
  • Tomator Puree
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Whole spices
  • Ground Spices
  • Herbs
  • Fat&Dairy
  • Basmati Rice

Steps

  1. Prepare saffron-kewra water and chop veggie
  2. Saute the onions
  3. Cook biryani on low heat for 5-6 minutes
  4. Serve hot chicken biryani with your favourite chutney or raita
