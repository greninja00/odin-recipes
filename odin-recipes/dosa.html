Dosa

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Parboiled Rice (idli-dosa rice)
  • 3/4 Regular Rice
  • 1/2 cup Whole Urad Dal(without skin) or Split Urad Dal(black lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds(methi dana)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chana Dal (gram lentils) optional
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil, for shallow frying

Steps

  1. Take all the ingredients to prepare the dosa batter.
  2. Rinse both types of rice (regular rice and parboiled rice) together in water
  3. Rinse urad dal and chana dal together in water
  4. Drain water from urad dal in a small bowl and reserve it
  5. Add water as needed and grind until smooth and fluffy batter
  6. The batter should be fluffy and not very thick. Transfer it to a large container.
  7. Drain water from rice and add them to the same mixer grinder jar.
  8. Add water as needed and grind until smooth texture.
  9. Add salt and mix both batters well using spoon.
  10. During fermentation, the batter volume would increase and the tiny bubbles would appear on the surface when you stir it with a spoon.
  11. Heat non-stick tava or iron tava (skillet or griddle) over medium flame
  12. Apply 1-teaspoon oil (or ghee / butter for crispy dosa) around the edges of dosa (or spread oil/ghee/butter evenly with brush for crispy dosa).
  13. Cook until the bottom surface turns light brown and the edges start to come upward, it will take around 2-minutes
  14. Flip it and cook for a minute. If you are making thin dosa
