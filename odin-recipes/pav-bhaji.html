Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • 1 medium size Cauliflower, florets,
  • 1 Capsicum, roughly chopped,
  • ½ inch Ginger, slice,
  • 2-3 Garlic, chopped
  • Water
  • 5 medium Potato, cubed
  • 1 Carrots, pelled, roughly chopped,
  • ⅓ cup Green peas,
  • Salt to taste,
  • 2-3 tbsp Butter,
  • 1 cup Onion, chopped
  • 1 ½ cup Tomato, chopped,
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Coriander leaves,
  • Ladi pav
  • Lemon wedge

Steps

  1. In a large pot or pressure cooker add cauliflower, capsicum, ginger, garlic, water, potato, carrot, green peas, salt, butter then cover and cook until soft.
  2. Now mash everything properly with a masher and set aside.
  3. In a large tava or a shallow pan heat butter, oil then add onion and saute until translucent.
  4. Now add tomatoes, green chillies, ginger garlic paste, salt and saute until they are soft.
  5. Add fresh tomato puree and continue cooking for 6-8 minutes.
  6. Then add capsicum and continue cooking for another 2 minutes. Once done remove 2-3 tbsp masala aside for further use.
  7. Now, add degi red chilli powder, butter, pav bhaji masala and saute for a minute.
  8. Now, add boiled & mashed vegetables, butter, pav bhaji masala, water, sugar and continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes.
  9. Finish the pav bhaji with fresh coriander leaves and switch off the flames.
  10. Serve hot with toasted pav, onion, lemon wedges, coriander leaves.
  11. In a hot pan add butter degi red chilli powder and coat the pav in that inside out and toast it properly, set aside for further use.
